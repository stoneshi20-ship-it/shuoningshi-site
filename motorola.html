Motorola Razr+

A website redesign case study for the Razr+ × Pantone edition.

Feb 2024 · 1 wk UI/UX Design Design Research Client: Motorola

A one-week redesign sprint reimagining the Razr+ product page. The new flow streamlines a cluttered, repetitive spec layout — folding steps and showing only selectable items to keep attention on the purchase. A dynamic Pantone gallery uses video, stills, GIFs, and interactive visuals, while scroll-, click-, and hover-based interactions add playful depth to browsing.
