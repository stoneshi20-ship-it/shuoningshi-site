Product Design · Award-winning

AEGIS

The first ergonomic stationary bench designed specifically for shoulder training.

Feb 2023 – Oct 2024 · 20 wks Product Development Research & Testing Advisor: Prof. Suresh Sethi

Red Dot Winner 2025 · IDA Design Awards 2024 · NY Product Design Silver · IDEA

Free-weight training builds strength but carries real risk — especially for the shoulder. AEGIS sets the seat and backrest at 15° and 105° for optimal posture, with lumbar and headrest support that follows the spine. Three adjustable mechanisms can all be tuned while seated, so users of any body type find a safe, comfortable form in real time.
